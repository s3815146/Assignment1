INTEREST IN IT

I have been interested in various different aspects of IT since my early childhood. I was always the one to have the latest device or console and would know every detail about a piece of hardware that hadn't even released. My main interest has always been Apple and their products. My interest began around the time of Steve Jobs' death in 2011, when I read his biography and started taking a real interest in the company and what it stood for.

I chose to come to RMIT to study the Bachelor of Information technology as I wanted to learn a practical skill that I could use to do things myself in the real world, rather than wait for others to do so.

During my studies, I hope to gain us much programming and technical knowledge as possible, so that I can create my own solutions without needing the assistance of others.
